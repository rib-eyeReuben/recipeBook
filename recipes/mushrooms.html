Bánh mì Mushrooms



Description

This delicious mushroom dish is a Munk family classic. Inspired by a vegetarian Banh Mi recipe. Stun your friends and family with this simple, easy to make, delicious mushroom dish that goes well with any carbohydrate. Is your pasta lacking flavor? Add these mushrooms! Want to add variety to your sandwiches? Add these mushrooms! Any life problem can be fixed by these wonders. Try it today with just 4 easy to come by ingredients.


    Ingredients
  • Mushrooms of choice
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
    Steps
  1. Cut mushrooms top to bottom into thin slices
  2. Put mushrooms on a pan on low until soft
  3. Empty water (from mushrooms) from pan and put sesame oil, soy sauce, and minced garlic
  4. Cook on medium until soy sauce and sesame oil are almost all gone
