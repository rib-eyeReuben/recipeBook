Bánh mì Mushrooms


    Ingredients
  • Mushrooms of choice
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
    Steps
  1. Cut mushrooms top to bottom into thin slices
  2. Put mushrooms on a pan on low until soft
  3. Empty water (from mushrooms) from pan and put sesame oil, soy sauce, and minced garlic
  4. Cook on medium until soy sauce and sesame oil are almost all gone
